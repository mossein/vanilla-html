why this site?

In a world of algorithms and endless feeds, I wanted to make something simple. Something that loads fast and asks nothing of you except perhaps a moment of your attention.

No trackers. No cookies. No engagement metrics.

Just words and the space around them.

This site is built with pure HTML—no CSS, no JavaScript. It's an homage to the early web, when personal sites were little handmade houses on the digital frontier.
